My Dream Vacation

created by Sumaiyah Baksh

My grandpa told me we were going on my dream vacation. My heart filled with joy and so much anticipation.
He said we'll eat our favorite foods and laugh all day, sharing smiles and stories along the way.
We'll swim together underneath the warm sun, splashing in the water and having so much fun.
I closed my eyes and smiled, imagining it all, my grandpa right beside me, catching me if I fall.
But I woke up from my slumber and felt the pain inside, because my dream vacation left when my grandpa died.
